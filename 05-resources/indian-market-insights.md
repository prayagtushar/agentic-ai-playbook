### Tips for Global Remote
1. **Strong portfolio**: Projects > degrees
2. **Communication**: Clear written English
3. **Overlap hours**: Be available 4-6 hrs overlap
4. **Rate negotiation**: Charge $30-80/hr based on experience
5. **Tax**: Consult a CA for international income
