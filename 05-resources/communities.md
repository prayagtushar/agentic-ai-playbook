## How to Engage

1. **Start with Discord**: Most active, beginner-friendly
2. **Share your projects**: Build in public
3. **Answer questions**: Teaching reinforces learning
4. **Attend meetups**: Network with local builders
5. **Participate in hackathons**: Learn by building under pressure
