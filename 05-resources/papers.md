## How to Read Papers

1. **Read abstract + conclusion first**: Know the main contribution
2. **Skip math (initially)**: Focus on intuition and results
3. **Take notes**: One paragraph summary per paper
4. **Connect to code**: How does this relate to frameworks you use?
5. **Discuss**: Explain the paper to someone (or write about it)
